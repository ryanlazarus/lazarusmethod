How It Works
Nutrition does not have to be complicated. There are only three things you can change about the way you eat: what you eat, how much you eat, and when you eat. That is E, A, and T.
Below, set your priorities. Pick the lever you will always focus on, the one you will focus on often, and the one you will work on sometimes. As you select each lever, practical recommendations will appear below showing you exactly how to apply it. At the end, you will see what a complete day looks like based on your selections.
The Three Levers
E
Essentials
Modify what you eat
Are you eating the right foods? Whole proteins, healthy fats, vegetables, fruits. This lever is about food quality. Swap the processed stuff for real food.
A
Amount
Modify how much you eat
Are your portions right? Too much of even good food works against you. This lever is about quantity. Eat enough to fuel your body, not overload it.
T
Timing
Modify when you eat
When are you eating? Late-night snacking, skipping breakfast, eating too close to bed. This lever is about your eating window and rhythm.
