Today's Intentions

Visualize Your Day — The 4 Quarters

Instructions: Visualize yourself making the right health decisions in each quarter of your day. See yourself implementing your intentions in vivid detail: where you are, what it looks like, how it feels to follow through. The more clear and specific your visualization, the more effective it will be. When you have already seen yourself making the right choice, the real moment becomes a replay, not a decision.
Q1
Morning
4 AM — 10 AM
The opening quarter. You set the tone here. Visualize yourself executing your morning routine and making your first healthy decisions of the day.
Q2
Midday
10 AM — 2 PM
Halftime check-in. Visualize yourself staying locked in on your intentions through meals, meetings, and the middle of your day.
Q3
Afternoon
2 PM — 6 PM
The stretch run. Energy shifts, routines get tested. Visualize yourself holding the line on your intentions when it would be easiest to let them slide.
Q4
Evening
6 PM — 10 PM
The closing quarter. Visualize yourself winding down with purpose: a nourishing dinner, quality time, and a wind-down routine that sets up tomorrow.
