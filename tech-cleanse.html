Cleanse Steps

01 News Detox ✓ Complete

Detox from the continuous news and propaganda cycle that fuels fear and anxiety. While staying informed is important, the constant influx of news — especially negative news — can lead to increased levels of stress, fear, and anxiety.

02 Social Media Fast ✓ Complete

Take a break from the posts, likes, and alerts that distract you from your priorities. Social media platforms are engineered to capture your attention — the endless scrolling and notifications pull you away from real-life priorities and can lead to feelings of inadequacy.

03 Contact Purge ✓ Complete

Remove 10 contacts from your phone that no longer serve your health or goals. Over time we accumulate contacts that no longer align with our current life or aspirations, creating invisible digital weight.

04 App Rearrangement & Deletion ✓ Complete

Delete all unused apps and rearrange the remaining ones to prioritize what truly matters. What lives on your home screen is a direct reflection of what you are inviting into your attention every day.

BONUS 24-Hour Phone Removal ✓ Complete

Experience your life untethered and unfiltered without your phone for an entire day. A full day without it offers a powerful perspective on how reliant we have become on a single device for our sense of connection, productivity, and identity.
