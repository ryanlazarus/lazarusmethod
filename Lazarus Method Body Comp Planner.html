How to Use This Tool

This tool builds a personalized body composition plan designed to reduce your body fat percentage while preserving your lean muscle mass and hydration. It is not a weight loss calculator. The goal is to change the composition of your body, not just the number on the scale.

1 Enter your information above. Your name, date, sex, age, current weight, and current body fat percentage are needed to calculate your baseline. If you do not know your body fat percentage, ask your provider for a measurement.

2 Choose your plan settings. Select the food plan your provider has recommended, your protein target (grams per pound of body weight), your goal body fat percentage, your daily calorie deficit (300 to 600 calories), your desired timeline in weeks, how many meals you eat per day, and your general activity level.

3 Click Generate Plan. Your personalized report will appear below with daily calorie and macronutrient targets broken down by meal. To save or print your plan, click Print / Save PDF.
