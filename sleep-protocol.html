How to Use This Tool

This tool creates your personalized Optimal Sleep Protocol by reverse-engineering the ideal bedtime from the time you want to wake up. It then builds a structured evening wind-down routine and morning routine around your sleep window, giving you a clear plan for every 30-minute block from 8 PM to 8 AM.

1 Enter your information above. Your name, date, age, and current average sleep hours establish your baseline. If you are unsure of your current average, estimate the number of hours you typically sleep on a weeknight.

2 Set your sleep targets. Choose the time you want to wake up each morning, how many hours of sleep you are targeting, how long you need to wind down before bed, and your caffeine and screen cutoff preferences.

3 Click Generate Protocol. Your personalized routine will appear below with every 30-minute block mapped out, color-coded by activity type. To save or print your protocol, click Print / Save PDF.
