The Nourish Target

How to Read the Target

Think of this like an archery target. The green center is the bullseye where the most nutrient-dense, highest-quality foods live. The yellow ring contains foods that are good but not optimal. The red outer ring is the danger zone where heavily processed, inflammatory, and nutrient-poor foods sit. The closer you eat to the center, the better you fuel your body.

Your Challenge

Remove all red-zone foods for at least one full day. Just one day. Look at the outer ring of each macronutrient section below and commit to avoiding those foods for 24 hours. Track your red-free days below. Once one day feels easy, try two in a row. Then three. Build momentum.
