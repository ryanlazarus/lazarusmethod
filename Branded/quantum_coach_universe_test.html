Your Quantum Coach is your personal coach who is strategically presenting you with challenges so you can successfully execute your defensive gameplan. By viewing life's inevitable setbacks as purposefully designed tests, this mindset allows you to approach difficulties with a proactive and positive attitude.

Complete this worksheet to develop the mental tenacity needed to triumph over adversity. Your reframes and strategies will build into a personalized resilience gameplan.

Section 1 — Identify Your Challenge

Describe one recent challenge, problem, or setback you have faced. Then select or write the emotions it triggered. Naming the emotion is the first step to depersonalizing the challenge.

Section 2 — Reframe as a Universe Test

Now reframe this challenge as a Universe Test from your Quantum Coach. The challenge carries over automatically. Choose how you see it — then write your intentional response.
