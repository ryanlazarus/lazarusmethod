The Seven Chakras
The chakra system maps seven energy centers in the body, running from the base of the spine to the crown of the head. Each one governs a different dimension of your physical, emotional, and mental health. When a chakra is balanced, the energy flows freely and that area of your life feels stable. When it's blocked or underactive, you feel the effects in ways most people don't connect back to the source.
The Lazarus Method has seven health elements: Nourish, Move, Rest, Connect, Learn, Spark, and Challenge. As you work through this assessment, start to notice how closely these ancient energy centers mirror the areas of modern health you're already working on. At the end, you'll make those connections yourself.
