The 80/20 Rule
80% nutrient-dense foods. 20% treats. No guilt. Just balance.
⚖️
How It Works
For each day of the week, answer three simple questions. Did you eat a protein? Did you eat a fruit or vegetable? Did you have a treat? Tap the ones that apply. The emojis are not meant to be the exact foods you ate. They represent the type of choice you made. Pick whichever one is closest.
This is not about being perfect. Nobody eats perfectly, and that is not the goal. The goal is to see where your week actually lands. Be honest. If you had pizza on Friday, tap it. If you skipped veggies on Saturday, leave it blank. At the bottom, your weekly score will show you how close you are to 80/20. That is the target. Not 100/0. Just 80% good choices, 20% treats. That is a life you can sustain.
