How the Pyramid Works
Nature exposure is not all-or-nothing. Like a pyramid, the foundation is built on small daily doses. The higher you go, the less frequent but more immersive the experience becomes. Open each tier below, plan how you will integrate nature at that level, and check it off when you do it. Start at the base and work your way up.
