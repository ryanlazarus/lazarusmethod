Why Fast?

Fasting is not starvation. It is a controlled, intentional pause that activates repair systems your body cannot access while it is digesting food. When you stop eating for a defined period, your body shifts from burning glucose to burning fat, triggers cellular cleanup called autophagy, reduces systemic inflammation, and sharpens mental clarity.

These benefits are time-dependent. The longer you fast, the deeper the repair. This tool tracks your progress and shows you exactly what is happening inside your body at each stage.

Medical Disclaimer

Fasting is not appropriate for everyone. Before beginning any fasting protocol, consult with Dr. Lazarus or your Lazarus Method health coach to determine which fasting interval is safe and appropriate for your individual health status, medications, and goals. Do not fast if you are pregnant, nursing, under 18, or have a history of disordered eating. If you feel faint, dizzy, or unwell at any point during a fast, end the fast immediately and eat.
