There is something powerful about the moment you finally write down what you want for your health. It stops being a wish and becomes a plan. This is that moment.

Three focused questions are all it takes to turn a vague intention into a real path forward. And with The Lazarus Method behind you, you are not doing this alone. Every goal you set here becomes part of the roadmap your practitioner uses to guide, support, and celebrate your progress with you.
