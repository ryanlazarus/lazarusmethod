The Challenge
The elements at the top of the framework, Learn, Connect, Challenge, and Spark, are what make a healthy life a meaningful one. New adventures are the fastest way to activate all four at once.
Why New Adventures Matter
Routine is the enemy of growth. The same environment, the same people, and the same schedule will produce the same thinking. Adventures interrupt that pattern and build the kind of confidence that only comes from doing something you have never done before.
How to Use This Worksheet
Plan your next adventure. Describe what you will do, what you hope to learn from it, and rate how far outside your comfort zone it takes you.
