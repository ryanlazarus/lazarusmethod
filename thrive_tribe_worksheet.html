The Lazarus Method Thrive Tribe is your personal health community dedicated to fostering a collaborative circle of individuals with shared health goals and interests. This serves as your health support network, where not only family and friends but also acquaintances and globally-connected, like-minded individuals can collectively support your health goals and interests.

This worksheet is designed to help you identify your health goals, define your community, and build an actionable engagement plan for your Thrive Tribe.
